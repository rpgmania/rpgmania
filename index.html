Combat Tips for GM

  • The GM starts the session with two fear tokens.
  • The GM moves when a player:
    • Rolls with Fear.
    • Rolls a Failure.
    • Takes consequential actions.
    • Gives you a golden opportunity.
    • Turns to you for what happens next.
  • GM can act post-player turn if at least 1 token in action tracker to activate monsters (attack). E.g., 2 tokens allow 2 monster actions. Fear in monsters allows regular attacks.
  • If Fear is available when the action tracker appears, spend some to add tokens.
  • Spend 2 Fear to activate adversaries first, useful for ambushes or powerful adversaries.
  • If no Fear, convert 2 character tokens on Action Tracker to 1 Fear.
