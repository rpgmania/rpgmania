GM Moves

  • The GM starts the campaigns with two fear tokens and stack from session to session.
  • The GM moves when a player:
    • Rolls with Fear.
    • Rolls a Failure.
    • Takes consequential actions.
    • Gives you a golden opportunity.
    • Turns to you for what happens next.

Combat Tips for GM

  • If Fear is available when the action tracker appears, spend some to add tokens. Spend 2 Fear to activate adversaries first, useful for ambushes or powerful adversaries.
  • If no Fear, convert 2 character tokens on Action Tracker to 1 Fear.
  • Keep some fear tokens for the special abilities of the adversaries
  • You may spend two Fear to interrupt between PCs acting and make a GM move as if they had rolled a failure or with Fear.
